About EduLoca

EduLoca is a platform created to empower both learners and instructors. We believe that knowledge should be accessible to everyone, and teaching should be a flexible way to earn and grow.

Our community brings together students, professionals, and stay-at-home parents—all eager to share their expertise or learn new skills. Whether it's academic help, hobbies, or career development, EduLoca connects people for focused, 30-minute tutoring sessions based on real-time needs.

With simple app-based scheduling, video calls, and review tracking, we ensure convenience and trust. Our future plans include city-wise subject discovery, group learning options, and AI-based tutor matching to personalize experiences even further.

Our mission is to democratize education—one street, one session, one learner at a time.
